Our Family Story

Welcome to the Marshina Family website – a place where our family's love, legacy, and laughter come together. We're proud to call Marlton, New Jersey our home, and we cherish every moment spent here.

Our family is made up of seven strong, unique, and kind-hearted individuals: Crystal, Bill (William), Andrew, Giada, Gabby, and Jacob. Each person brings something special to the family that keeps our home full of energy, fun, and love.

Our Home

Our home at 64 Colts Gait Road is more than just an address – it's where countless memories have been made. From backyard barbeques and movie nights to holiday celebrations and quiet Sundays, it’s a place filled with warmth and tradition.

Located in a beautiful, peaceful neighborhood, our house is a sanctuary. We love decorating it for every season, maintaining a smart home system to make life easier, and filling it with love and laughter.

Our Traditions

The Marshina Family has some awesome traditions. Every Christmas, we go all out with lights, cookies, and matching pajamas. Sundays are for family dinners – no phones, just great food and time together. Birthdays are huge in our house, and vacations always involve laughs, spontaneous road trips, and a lot of pictures.

What We Value

Family first – always. We value honesty, hard work, fun, and taking care of one another. Whether it's helping each other with school, home projects, or just spending time together, being close-knit is what keeps us strong.
